Bicep Curl
Bicep curls strengthen the arm by flexing the elbow through repetitive resistance movements.
Double Bicep
Double bicep curls enhance arm strength by engaging both biceps simultaneously through resistance and flexion.
Jumping Jacks
Jumping jacks are a full-body exercise that improves cardiovascular fitness through dynamic jumping and arm-leg coordination.
Pushup
Pushups are a bodyweight exercise that strengthens the chest, shoulders, and triceps by lowering and lifting the body in a plank position.
Squat
Squats are a lower-body exercise that targets the thighs, hips, and glutes by bending the knees and lowering the body into a seated position.
Lunges
Lunges are a lower-body exercise that strengthens the legs and glutes by stepping forward and lowering the body until both knees are bent at 90 degrees.
Planks
A core-strengthening exercise where you hold a pushup-like position, maintaining a straight body line from head to heels to build abdominal and back strength.
Strict Push-Up
A strict push-up is a bodyweight exercise that strengthens the chest, shoulders, and triceps by lowering and raising the body while maintaining a straight line and proper form.
Wide Hands Push-Up
A push-up variation with hands placed wider than shoulder-width apart, targeting the chest and shoulders more intensively.
Pike Push-Up
A push-up variation where the body forms an inverted V shape, with hips raised high and feet closer to the hands, focusing on the shoulders and upper chest.
SphinX Push-Up
A push-up variation performed with forearms on the ground and elbows close to the body, emphasizing triceps and shoulder strength.
Side Lying Leg Lift
An exercise performed while lying on one side, lifting the top leg upwards to strengthen the hip abductors and outer thighs.
High Knees
A cardio exercise where you jog in place, lifting your knees as high as possible toward your chest to improve cardiovascular fitness and leg strength.
Leg Raises
Leg raises strengthen the lower abs by lifting legs while lying on your back.
Basic Plank
The basic plank strengthens the core by holding a push-up position with a straight body.
Knee Plank
The knee plank is a modified plank where you hold the position on your knees instead of toes, reducing intensity.
Side Plank
The side plank strengthens the obliques by balancing on one side with legs stacked and body straight.
Wall Sit
The wall sit strengthens the legs by sliding down a wall into a sitting position and holding it.
Reverse Lunges
Reverse lunges target the legs and glutes by stepping backward into a lunge and returning to the start position.
Leg Press
The leg press strengthens the lower body by pushing a weighted platform away with your feet while seated.
Deadlift
The deadlift builds strength by lifting a weighted barbell from the ground to hip level while maintaining a straight back.
Dumbbell Press
The dumbbell press strengthens the chest, shoulders, and triceps by pressing dumbbells upward from shoulder height while lying on a bench.
Shoulder Press
The shoulder press targets the shoulders and triceps by pressing weights overhead while standing or sitting.
Hyperextension
Hyperextensions strengthen the lower back by bending forward at the waist and then extending back to a neutral position.
Leg Curl
The leg curl targets the hamstrings by curling the legs upward against resistance while lying face down or sitting.
